Privacy Policy

Voice Journal

Effective Date: April 17, 2026

Voice Journal ("the App") is developed by iamgroot. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being fully transparent about our practices.

1 Information We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. Voice Journal operates entirely on your device.

  • Voice recordings: The App uses your device's built-in speech recognition service to convert speech to text in real time. Audio is processed locally by Android's on-device speech recognizer or by Google's speech recognition service (depending on your device configuration). We do not record, store, or transmit any audio data ourselves.
  • Notes: All notes you create (titles and content) are stored locally on your device in an internal database. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

2 Permissions

The App requests the following permission:

  • Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO): Required to capture your voice for speech-to-text conversion. This permission is only used when you actively start a voice recording session. No audio is recorded, stored, or transmitted by the App.

3 Third-Party Services

The App relies on Android's built-in SpeechRecognizer API for voice-to-text conversion. Depending on your device and settings, speech recognition may be processed:

  • On-device: If you have offline speech recognition language packs installed, audio is processed entirely on your device.
  • Via Google's servers: If offline recognition is unavailable, your device may send audio to Google's speech recognition servers for processing. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy, not ours.

We do not integrate any analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services.

4 Data Storage and Security

  • All note data is stored in the App's private internal storage on your device, which is sandboxed by Android and inaccessible to other apps.
  • If you use the Export feature, a zip file is created in the App's temporary cache and shared via Android's share sheet. The temporary file is managed by the operating system.
  • Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all stored notes.

5 Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any of your data to third parties. Your notes remain entirely under your control.

6 Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The App does not collect personal information from any user.

7 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
